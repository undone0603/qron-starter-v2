How AuthiChain + QRON Eliminates Counterfeits

1

Proof-of-Origin at Manufacturing

Every product gets a unique QRON code — a living QR identity that's blockchain-anchored. This record links to SKU, batch, timestamp, and certifications. It cannot be forged or tampered with.
